Stardew Valley Mod

The mod allows sprinklers water garden pots and pet bowls automatically at the start of the day.

To water pet bowl, the sprinkler must reach the bowl area of the pet bowl.

Sprinkler with enricher will fertilize the pot.


Config
  Enable Mod:
  - Enable or disable mod.

  Water Indoor:
  - Enable or disable sprinkler from watering garden pots located indoors.

  Water Pet Bowl:
  - Enable or disable sprinkler from filling pet bowl.
